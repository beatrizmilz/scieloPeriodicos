About the journal

 

Basic information

 

The first edition of the "Sanitary Engineering Journal" was edited by the Brazilian Section of the Associação Interamericana de Engenharia Sanitária e Ambiental - AIDIS, (Inter-American Association of Sanitary and Environmental Engineering), in June 1962, with the objective of updating the technical culture of professionals in the field of sanitary and environmental engineering.

The Brazilian Section of AIDIS develops and transforms itself into the Associação Brasileira de Engenharia Sanitária e Ambiental - ABES the (Brazilian Association of Sanitary and Environmental Engineering), in June 1966, maintaining continuity with the Edition of the "Sanitary Engineering Journal".

Over the years, the format of the publication went through some changes and in 1996 it began to be called "Revista Engenharia Sanitária e Ambiental" with an exclusive content of articles of a technical and scientific nature.

Its abbreviated title is Eng. Sanit. Ambient., and it should be used in bibliographies, footnotes, references and bibliographic strips.

Instructions to authors

 

Objective and scope

 

The Revista Engenharia Sanitária e Ambiental aims to publish original technical and scientific articles in the areas of sanitation and the environment and their interfaces. The current areas of interest of Revista Engenharia Sanitária e Ambiental (ESA) are as follows:

  • Development of environmental technologies (water, sewage, solid waste and air);
  • Management of solid, liquid and gaseous waste, sludge etc.;
  • Resource recovery (water, sludge, nutrients, energy, carboxylic acids, etc.);
  • Environmental remediation and monitoring (water, soil and air);
  • Systems of environmental sizing, modeling and optimization (water, sewage, solid waste, sludge, air and soil);
  • Rational use of water and energy;
  • Rural sanitation;
  • Public health and environmental microbiology;
  • Environmental analysis and assessment (assessment of environmental impact, risk analysis, life cycle analysis, material flow analysis, etc.);
  • Social, economic and political aspects of environmental management and basic sanitation management.

 

RESA does not accept manuscripts predominantly focused on the following topics:

  • Simulation/modeling oriented towards mathematical issues;
  • Reactions/processes oriented towards chemical issues;
  • Synthesis of materials;
  • Reuse of waste oriented towards the area of Soil Science;
  • Application of renewable energy, without close connection with the area of Sanitary and Environmental Engineering;
  • Studies on monitoring of species.
 

 

Submission of contributions

 

Contributions must be exclusively registered by accessing the Electronic Scientific Online Library (Scielo) ScholarOne system, via the link https://mc04.manuscriptcentral.com/esa-scielo. Registrations of articles by any other form of submission will not be accepted.

Upon article submission, the author will receive a confirmation email, along with an identification code.

The author will be able to follow the entire evaluation process by the Scielo ScholarOne system.

For clarification of any additional questions, please email esa@abes-dn.org.br, always including the reference number of the submitted article.

 

 

Fees for authors

 

The manuscripts must be submitted only written in English.

ESA charges a submission fee of BRL 200.00 (two hundred reais), which must be included in the act of submission as an attachment (complementary file that is NOT for evaluation). The evaluation process will only start after the editorial team has verified proof of payment of the submission fee.

The fee is intended for nonmembers of ABES. If the main author is a member, attach the document containing the ABES or CPF registration number and full name (complementary file that is NOT for evaluation).

If you have any questions, please email esa@abes-dn.org.br.

Become a member of ABES: http://socio.abes-dn.org.br/
