About the journal

 

Basic information

 

Estudos Econômicos publishes original manuscripts in all areas of economics, written by Brazilian and foreign researchers. Created in 1971, Estudos Econômicos is published every trimester by the Department of Economics of the School of Economics, Business, Accounting and Actuary of the University of Sao Paulo.

Its abbreviated title is Estud. Econ., which should be used in bibliographies, footnotes and bibliographical references and strips.

Instructions to authors

 

Scope and editory policy

 

Estudos Econômicos aims to publish manuscripts resulting from original research and with relevant contribution to the Literature. Texts can be written in Portuguese or English.

If the Editorial Board’s members consider the manuscript aligned to the journal’s scope and requirements, it is forwarded to ad hoc referees following the double blind review system whereby authors do not get to know who their referees are and vice-versa.

Estudos Econômicos also publishes book reviews.

The texts of the articles and the book reviews should be presented with 1,5 line, font size Times New Roman 12, and minimum margins of 2.5 cm. Articles and book reviews should not exceed 30 and 6 pages, respectively.

The edited version of the article or book review accepted for publication is forwarded to its authors, who should double-check them up to the established deadline, after which it will be considered that the authors agree with the edited version. 

Estudos Econômicos does not charge submission fee nor publication fee, reason for which authors who submit manuscripts to Estudos Econômicos should colaborate, if invited, by reviewing manuscripts.

 

 

Manuscripts' form and presentation

 

Manuscripts should not exceed 30 pages and be presented with 1,5 line space, font size Times New Roman 12, and minimum margins of 2.5 cm.

They must include, both in Portuguese and English, an abstract of no more than 150 words, 3 to 5 keywords, and the JEL classification codes. Pages should be numbered.

Articles with mathematical formulas developed by the authors must present the deduction.

References should include only the works quoted along the article and be presented in alphabetical order. References should comply with the Chicago quotation style.

Files for the texts and table should be in WORD and PDF (Acrobat Reader).Graphics and figures should be presented in Excel files with all the relevant information. 

 

 

The sending of manuscripts

 

Submissions should be made electronically in the following address:

www.revistas.usp.br/ee
