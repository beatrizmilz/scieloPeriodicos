About the journal

 

Basic information

 

Educar em Revista is an open access journal of the Department of Education at Universidade Federal do Paraná (Brazil) with continuous online publication designed to disseminate original scientific papers in Portuguese, Spanish, English, French, German or Italian that address themes related to Education. The texts should be submitted to Educar em Revista through the Electronic Journals System (SER) at http://revistas.ufpr.br/educar select "Cadastro (cadastrar-se como autor)” (Register as an author).

Educar em Revista was named Revista de Educação in 1977 and 1978. Both editions are available at:

1978 - http://ojs.c3sl.ufpr.br/ojs2/index.php/educar/issue/view/1556
1977 - http://ojs.c3sl.ufpr.br/ojs2/index.php/educar/issue/view/1555

The journal's title abbreviation is Educ. rev., that must be used in bibliographic lists, footnotes, references and bibliographic labels.

Instructions to authors

 

Scope and Policies

 

Educar em Revista publishes original articles on education. Its purpose is to address current and significant issues to understand educational phenomena, and it accepts papers related to the field of Education with the following requirements:

I - Articles should not have been published in other journals or meeting proceedings.

II - They should be written in one of the following languages: Portuguese, English, Spanish, French, German, or Italian.

III - They should have up to three (3) authors:

a) For a single author, the author should have a PhD title.

b) If there are coauthors, one (1) of the authors can be a doctoral student, and the other authors must have a PhD degree.

c) Articles by graduates and masters will not be accepted by the journal.

IV - They should contain results of investigation in Education, explicitly showing the objectives, procedures and analyses and should not be just a report of experiences or literature review.

V - They should follow the formal aspects described in the Guidelines for authors.

VI. At submission, articles should not be identified with the names of the authors in the original text. The author(s) of the manuscript should include their full names exclusively on the field provided in the submission system, and they must also inform their education level, their ORCID ID, their affiliation, link to their CV (lattes for Brazilian authors) and a summary of their biography.

VII. The author is exclusively responsible for the opinions and perspectives expressed in the text, as well as for the accuracy and validity of the citations.

VIII. Since 1st August 2019, due to the internationalization policy, we recommend that articles by Brazilian authors submitted to the journal - to the continuous demand section - if approved, be translated into a foreign language (which could be English, Spanish, French, Italian or German). Any costs associated with translation and its respective proofreading will be the responsibility of the author(s). Educar em Revista will publish both versions of the text (in Portuguese and in the foreign language chosen by the author). The translated text should include the credits of the person in charge of the translation (name and e-mail address).

IX. If the manuscript is approved, the author will be exclusively responsible for the proofreading and formatting services. A proof of the provision of these services must be presented to Educar em Revista.  The Journal may suggest registered professionals who provide manuscript proofreading and formatting services. Exceptional cases will be evaluated by the editorial board.

X. Papers submitted simultaneously to other national or international journals will not be accepted.

Manuscripts that do not meet the editorial requirements of the Journal will be returned to the author(s). This will include papers that do not address a key issue in education; that do not present significant contributions to the analysis of the addressed theme. Regarding the articles, the following manuscripts will also be rejected: manuscripts that are merely descriptive, without conducting a theoretical or methodological analysis of the issue addressed; reports of experience or fragments of dissertations and theses; manuscripts that do not present empirical elements or arguments that are sufficiently developed to support their analyses and conclusions.

The Editorial Board can decide to publish the article, document, or interview to order, with the purpose of contributing to a more pressing debate in the field. This article or document will be evaluated by the Editorial Board.

Ethics policy:

Educar em Revista is committed to the ethical issues related to published documents and their review process, including the management of manuscripts, involving editors, reviewers, and authors. In this sense, the Journal distinguishes itself for its respect for ethical conduct, following the recommendations of the Committee on Publication Ethics - COPE.

The purpose of the journal is to promote the expansion of open science practices, with authors stating in their manuscripts the sources, methods and analytical treatment used to produce their results. The journal is engaged in a constant dialogue with authors, reviewers, and readers, responding to the need for revisions or clarifications.

Editors are attentive and intervene in the editorial process if there is a conflict of interest by reviewers or the editorial team.

The decision on whether to publish an article is based on principles of ethics in research and dissemination of information, in compliance with current regulations and good conduct in academic culture.

The identity of authors and reviewers will remain anonymous throughout the editorial process and after its completion.

Authors are fully responsible for the published texts.

Educar em Revista protects its editorial process from commercial or financial interests.

Ethical decisions and procedures are based on the following documents:

  • Resolução CNS nº 466/2012 (Ética na Pesquisa com seres humanos);
  • Resolução CNS nº 510/2016 (Ética na Pesquisa em Ciências Humanas e Sociais);
  • Manual da APA (Editora Penso, 2012);
  • Documento do CNPq - Ética e integridade na prática científica;
  • Conselho Nacional de Desenvolvimento Científico e Tecnológico (CNPq). Relatório da Comissão de Integridade de Pesquisa do CNPq;
  • Documento Ética e Pesquisa em Educação - v. 1 (Anped). 
  • “Principles of Transparency and Good Practice in Academic Publications” recommended by the Committee on Publication Ethics (COPE);
  • CSE’s White Paper on Promoting Integrity in Scientific Journal Publications [COUNCIL OF SCIENCE EDITORS]; ABEC BRASIL. Diretrizes do CSE para promover integridade em publicações de periódicos científicos, 2012.
  • DECLARATION ON RESEARCH ASSESSMENT (DORA). San Francisco.
  • Guia de Boas Práticas para o Fortalecimento da Ética na Publicação Cientifica.

Areas of interest: Education (Foundations of Education, Educational Administration, Educational Planning and Evaluation, Educational Policy, Teaching-Learning, Learning Assessment, Curriculum, Specific Topics in Education).

Peer review: Submitted papers will be sent to two ad hoc reviewers who have a national or international PhD title and a recognized production in the field of assessment. The names of the authors and reviewers will remain anonymous throughout the communication process in the Electronic Journals System. The aspects that guide the assessment of the original papers sent to the peers for review are text structure organization, command of scientific literature, text production that is fluent, cohesive, and suitable for academic writing, in addition to general issues related to the object of the study and the theories and methods presented in the article.

The review report is a brief text with remarks on the submitted paper that is entered in the system. The review report should say

a) Accepted; b) Mandatory corrections; c) Submit again for evaluation; d) Submit to another journal; e) Reject; f) See comments. If the reports are divergent, the article will be sent to a third reviewer.

The journal annually publishes the names of its ad hoc reviewers.

Journal audience: Researchers in education and related fields, teachers from different levels of education, graduate, and undergraduate students.

Archiving: Educar em Revista adopts the LOCKSS system, a free software developed by the Stanford University Library, which allows to preserve online journals chosen when examining their pages for recently published content and filing it. Each file is frequently validated against copies by other libraries. In case the content is damaged or lost, copies will be used for restoring.

Charge for submission and publishing: Educar em Revista has no charge for texts submitted to evaluation by its reviewers, as well as it has no payment for the process of publication (review, formatting, printing and distribution). Because this is a periodical of free access, there is also no charge for accessing or downloading.

 

 

Format and preparation of manuscripts

 

Articles submitted for publication at Educar em Revista will be previously assessed by the Editorial Board. The articles that do not meet the editorial requirements of the Journal will be returned and the others will be sent for analysis to the reviewers.

Articles must follow the formal aspects listed below:

I - Be according to the focus and scope of the journal;

II - Comply with all guidelines regarding the nature of the manuscript.

III - Meet the requirements set for author’s profile and conditions.

IV - After having a manuscript accepted for publication at Educar em Revista, authors should wait 24 months before submitting another manuscript.

V - Have a length of up to 20 (twenty) pages, including the title, abstract, keywords, images, tables, and bibliographic references;

VI - Have an abstract of up to 230 (two hundred and thirty) words in Portuguese and English and, if that is the case, also in the specific language (English, Spanish, French, Italian and German);

VII - Have up to 05 (five) key words in Portuguese and English, and, if applicable, also in another language;

VIII - Be in DOC file format, with Times New Roman font, size 12, space 1.5, A4 paper, 2.5 cm margins.

IX - Have identification on top of the page, in bold, with the title of the article in Portuguese and English, and if that is the case, also in a specific language. For funded research, the funding agency should be mentioned on a footnote.

X- Educar em Revista adopts procedures that prevent conflicts of interest during the assessment of the submitted articles. To prevent the identification of the author, no explicit mention should be made of the author in the body of the text, footnotes, or citations. We recommend the use of the “XXX” format to replace the author’s name and the work title. If it is approved for publication, the editorial team will contact you to retrieve the information that was anonymous.

XI - Footnotes should be used for necessary explanations. The use of bibliographic notes is not allowed, and references should be identified in the text, for example: (SURNAME, year, p. - page).

XII - Figures and tables (300 DPIs) should be sent in the text. The number and title should be written above the figure or table (For example: Figure 1 - Framework of Education Policies). The source should be informed below the figure or table (For example: Source: Museu Afro Brasil collection) and, if applicable, an explanation with up to 03 (three) lines.

XIII - References should follow current ABNT standards, with the first name (or the first two names in case of a compound name) of the author written in full. The title of the works and documents should be highlighted in italics as shown in the following examples:

SURNAME, First name.  Title: subtitle. 2. ed. (Edition). City: Publisher, year. (Name of the Collection - if any).

SURNAME, First name. Chapter Title. In: SURNAME, First name.  Book Title: subtitle. City: Publisher, year. p. (pages: first-last).

SURNAME, First name.  Book Title: subtitle. City: Publisher, year.

SURNAME, First name. Title of the article.  Name of the Journal, City, v. (volume), n. (number), p. (pages: first-last), year.

SURNAME, First name.  Title: subtitle. City, year. Available at: full electronic address. Accessed on Feb 15, 2021.

SURNAME, First name.  Title: subtitle. Thesis or Dissertation in (Program Area) - Institution, City, year.

In articles in languages other than Portuguese, references can follow the APA stand.

Submission of manuscripts

 

Educar em Revista accepts articles in Portuguese, English, Spanish, French, German, and Italian that have not been published previously that address themes related to education. The texts should be submitted to Educar em Revista through the Electronic Journals System (SER) at:
 http://revistas.ufpr.br/educar; select “Cadastro” to register as an author.

The manuscript should be directed to one of the following sections: Article, Thematic Dossier, Interview or Document.

I. Each article should have up to 20 (twenty) pages, including references, illustrations, graphs, maps, and tables. Abstract in the language of the article and in English, with up to 230 (two hundred and thirty) words. Up to five key words in the language of the article and in English. Text in Word for Windows according to the following recommendations: Times New Roman font, size 12, 1.5 space, A4 paper, 2.5 cm margins.

II. General information and metadata on the manuscript and the author(s) should be filled out in a specific section in the journal system at the time of submission.

III. All submission that do not comply with the standards will be returned and filed.
