About the journal

 

Basic Information

 

The journal Sequência (Florianópolis)  is bound to the Stricto Sensu Post-Graduation Course in Law from the Universidade Federal de Santa Catarina – UFSC, and publishes semiannually unpublished scientific works, both domestic and foreign, in thematic areas of Critic Theory of Law, emphasizing the contemporary constitutionalism and its reflexes in the following Law areas: Law, State and Society; Philosophy, Theory and History of Law; and International Relations. It was first published in 1980.

Its abbreviated title is Sequência (Florianópolis), which should be used in bibliographies, footnotes and bibliographical references and strips.

Peridiocity
The REVISTA SEQUÊNCIA - ESTUDOS JURÍDICOS E POLÍTICOS its publicated every four months, released in April, Agoust and December, published by the Graduate Program Stricto Sensu in Law from the Federal University of Santa Catarina - UFSC, available in digital version. Every year, are published at least 27 unpublished articles.

Instructions to authors

 

Scope and Policy

 

The journal Sequência (Florianópolis), bound to the Stricto Sensu Post-Graduation Course in Law from the Universidade Federal de Santa Catarina – UFSC, publishes unpublished articles, research reports, comunications in congresses,  written by teachers, students and researches bounded to post-graduation courses in Law, as well as book reviews, in thematic areas of Critic Theory of Law, emphasizing the contemporary constitutionalism and its reflexes in the following Law areas: Law, State and Society; Philosophy, Theory and History of Law; and International Relations.

 

 

Form and preparation of manuscripts

 

The articles submitted must be original and unpublished. The texts must be sent through the platform SEER (http://www.periodicos.ufsc.br/index.php/sequencia). Exceptionally, by e-mail to the e-mail sequencia@contato.ufsc.br. Preferably in RTF (rich text format).

The works must be written by teachers who hold the title of doctors, accepting as coauthors doctoral students and exceptionally masters.

Articles must have a minimum of 15 and maximum of 20 pages, typed in Times New Roman font, size 12, according to the spacing of 1.5, the top and left margin of 3 cm and the bottom and right margins of 2 cm. The foreign language articles will be published in the language of origin.

Papers will be accepted in Portuguese, English, Spanish, German, French and Italian. Abstract in Portuguese or in the source language and English, emphasizing objective, method and completion of the work. (maximum 100 words). Appointment of three keywords and / or phrases of the text, in Portuguese or in the source language, and English. The Portuguese texts should be written according to the standard presentation of articles of the Brazilian Association of Technical Standards - ABNT - NBR 6022, May 2003. The foreign articles should use the ISO equivalent. Citations in the text should be written according to NBR 10520/2002 or ISO equivalent. References to the sources cited should follow the standard NBR-6023/2002, ABNT, following the system AUTHOR / DATE.

Reviews should contain no more than two pages and are attached to the same methodological criteria for the article relatively to the type and size of the font and the line spacing.

The texts will be automatically rejected if they do not comply with the format required above.
The Journal sequence does not charge submission fees and article processing (APICS).

ATTENTION TO THE FOLLOWING COMMENTS:
- Articles in English (even by Brazilian authors, since linked to international institutions) will have preference in order of publication.
- Authors published in the journal should wait three years to have jobs again published.
- If the submitted article is accepted for publication in another journal while in the evaluation process in Revista Sequência, is the author of duty cancel sending your job or request the cancellation by email sequencia@contato.ufsc.br. Not done so and the article non-unpublished being accepted by our evaluators, will be considered as if the author had published their work in the Revista Sequência, which will result in a period of three years (from the accepted date of the work) until to publish again in the journal.

 

 

Send of the manuscripts

 

The originals can be sent to Universidade Federal de Santa Catarina, Centro de Ciências Jurídicas, sala 216, 2º andar, Campus Universitário Trindade. Caixa Postal 6510 - CEP 88036-970.
